FAQ

Why are notes missing sometimes?

Live capture depends on captions visibility and network delivery. Keep captions ON and MOM live hook active.

Can I share MoM publicly?

Yes. Generate a share link in Control Center and send the read-only URL.

Why is email queued?

Queue + retries improves reliability and provides status visibility in admin jobs.
